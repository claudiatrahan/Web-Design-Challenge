Latitude vs. Max Temperature

The plot above shows the relationship between the latitudes of over 500 cities with their maximum temperatures on May 6th, 2020. The plot shows a fairly strong correlation. As city latitudes approach 0 from both the northern and southern hemispheres, the maximum temperatures increase.
